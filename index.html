Your Space of Comfort and Harmony

The residential complex is located in the prestigious Nueva Andalucía, surrounded by greenery and golf courses. The architecture is designed to let residents enjoy the Mediterranean climate and panoramic views. Everything needed for a comfortable lifestyle is within easy reach. The interiors of the apartments combine modern design with functionality.

Facts and Figures

Property Types and Units

Magna Marbella is a prestigious residential complex offering apartments, penthouses, and duplexes. The complex features:

  • Apartments with 2, 3, 4, and 5 bedrooms
  • Penthouses with spacious terraces
  • Two-level duplex apartments

There are no villas or townhouses in the complex. All properties are located in modern residential buildings with elevators, underground parking, and landscaped grounds.

Apartment Sizes

  • 1-bedroom: 60–85 m²
  • 2-bedroom: 100–120 m²
  • 3-bedroom: 160–370 m²
  • 4-bedroom: 200–290 m²
  • 5-bedroom: 300–445 m²
  • Terraces: from 10 m² to 100 m² (depending on the type of property)

Purchase Prices

  • 1-bedroom: €320,000 – €400,000
  • 2-bedroom: €479,000 – €700,000
  • 3-bedroom: €1,190,000 – €1,850,000
  • 4–5-bedroom, penthouses and duplexes: €1,250,000 – €4,000,000
  • Average price per m²: €4,000 – €6,000

Magna Marbella — Comfort and Nature

An exclusive residential complex in the prestigious Nueva Andalucía, surrounded by golf courses and green hills. Spacious terraces, modern layouts, and panoramic views create a sense of light, openness, and constant connection with nature. Many apartments offer views of the sea and mountains.

The community is fully gated and secured, with architecture designed to let residents spend more time outdoors.

The atmosphere combines privacy and greenery with the convenience of nearby infrastructure and Puerto Banús.
